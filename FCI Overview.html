Project Roadmap

The Rebrand Journey

From vision to website to market. Every phase of the FCI rebranding project — what we built, the decisions we made, and what's next.

1
Starting Point
Vision
Improve branding, positioning, and marketing collateral to trigger growth. FCI's cybersecurity expertise needed a brand presence that matched — sharper identity, clearer messaging, stronger sales materials.
2
Phase 1 · Identity
Brand Foundation
Built the visual identity from scratch — logo, color palette, typography, voice guidelines, and imagery direction. Explored 10 font directions before landing on the final Inter Tight wordmark.
Brand Guidelines Logo Exploration
3
Phase 1 · Strategy
Messaging Architecture
Defined the ground truth for all copy — brand voice, buyer journey mapping, message hierarchy, competitive response framework, cost of inaction data, and channel adaptation. Then layered in FCI's decade-long AI story as a distinct narrative arc.
Messaging Architecture AI Messaging
4
Phase 2 · Copy
Website Copy
Translated the messaging architecture into complete, page-by-page website copy — homepage, three persona pages (broker-dealers & branch offices, insurance carriers & agencies, RIAs), six solution pages, and About. Ready for developer handoff.
Website Copy Question Layer
5
Phase 2 · Audit
Old Website Audit
Went through every page of the current fcicyber.com — 35+ news articles, 40 Cyber Bites, team bios, speaking appearances, media citations, service pages, and whitepapers. Each piece cataloged and marked: migrate, archive, or drop.
Content Migration Inventory
6
Phase 3 · Migration
Content Migration
Rewrote every piece of migrated content in the new brand voice — 8 leadership bios, 40 Cyber Bites, 18 speaking appearances, 16 media citations, 40 news articles, careers content, and contact information. All standardized and brand-aligned.
Company & Team Cyber Bites Speaking & Media News & Press
7
Phase 3 · Strategy
Positioning Decisions
Made the critical strategic calls — how to frame the Security Assessment offering, the question-layer messaging approach inspired by "They Ask, You Answer," and how FCI's AI capabilities weave through every touchpoint.
Security Assessment Question Layer AI Messaging
8
Phase 3 · Checkpoint
Review & Refinement
All brand assets, messaging, copy, and migrated content reviewed and refined before moving into the build phase. Every deliverable validated against the brand guidelines, messaging architecture, and market positioning.
We Are Here
9
Phase 4 · Build
Website Build
Architecture plan locked — 24-page sitemap, FastAPI + HTMX stack, 5-sprint build order. Hero design directions explored across 29 proposals and 5 final concepts. Prototype in progress.
Website Architecture Hero Gallery Five Hero Concepts Design Direction
10
Phase 4 · Sales Enablement
Collateral & Sales Enablement
Market-specific one-pagers for every audience, a 10-slide executive deck for board-level delivery, and a client-facing CISO Portal white paper. Plus the portal design reference itself.
Collaterals CISO Portal Design White Paper
11
Phase 5 · Growth
Email Sequences & Marketing Push To Do
Outbound email campaigns and sequences to drive the new positioning into the market. Targeted by persona — broker-dealers & branch offices, insurance carriers & agencies, and RIAs. Designed to convert awareness into conversations.
