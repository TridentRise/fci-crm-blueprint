After you submit, a member of the FCI team will reach out within one business day to schedule your gap analysis. There is no commitment, no contract, and no obligation beyond the conversation itself.

Breach or Security Incident
If you suspect a breach or active security incident

Call 973-227-8878 immediately and identify the call as a security incident. FCI's SOC team will initiate the incident response protocol, which includes device isolation, forensic evidence preservation, and coordinated communication with your compliance team.

Do not attempt to remediate the affected device yourself. Do not power off the device unless instructed to do so by the SOC team. Preserving the state of the device at the time of the incident is critical for forensic analysis and regulatory reporting.

Support Hours

FCI technical support is available during business hours, Eastern Time. The Security Operations Center (SOC) operates 24/7, 365 days per year — security alerts and incident response are never delayed by business hours.
