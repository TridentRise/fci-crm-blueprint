Aurora Orbs — Soft gradient atmosphere

Three large, deeply blurred color volumes — electric blue, sky, indigo — float behind the content with zero hard edges. In dark mode the effect references the aurora borealis: calm, enveloping, slightly mysterious. In light mode it shifts to a soft watercolor wash on pale paper — airy and clean, the aesthetic language of Stripe and Linear. The animation is continuous and subtle, giving the hero a sense of life without distraction. Stays entirely within the FCI palette. The lightest lift to implement.

Mood: invisible protection · always present · modern premium

Network Mesh — Living topology

Small glowing nodes drift slowly across the field. When two approach each other, a hairline edge forms between them — a living network graph in perpetual slow motion. Dark mode: deep space, glowing blue nodes, very cybersecurity-editorial. Light mode: pale background, navy nodes — reads immediately as a network architecture diagram or blueprint. The animation is meditative: nothing flashes, nothing demands attention. Directly renders FCI's core concept of endpoint visibility without a single literal image.

Mood: interconnected · always-on · watchful

Topographic — Contour lines, slow drift

Organic contour lines drift slowly across the right portion of the hero — like a surveyor's terrain map in motion. Dark mode: barely-there blue lines on near-black, very editorial. Light mode: deep navy lines on cream paper — looks like a beautiful cartographic reference, natural and precise. The metaphor pays off completely: "field," "terrain," "coverage," "knowing your exposure." Every word in FCI's vocabulary maps to cartography. Used by high-end data firms and editorial brands like Bloomberg. The most distinctive direction on this list.

Mood: systematic · field-aware · editorial precision

Data Stream — Compliance vocabulary as texture

Columns of slowly falling text — NIST, FINRA, SEC, MFA, EDR, hex codes, log identifiers — wash across the right half of the hero at 6% opacity. The intent is texture, not readability. The background whispers "constant monitoring, evidence at every level." Dark mode renders closer to the classic cybersecurity aesthetic but at a fraction of the opacity, refined rather than clichéd. Light mode is subtler still: faint navy on pale blue-white, almost like watermarked paper. A strong gradient keeps the foreground completely clean in both modes.

Mood: deep intelligence · constant monitoring · expert-level evidence

Prism Light — Crystalline geometry, single source

A single point of pure light emits two razor-sharp diagonal beams — like light splitting through the edge of cut glass or a crystal prism. A precision grid underlays the whole field. Pure CSS, zero images. Dark mode: electric beams of blue-white cutting through near-black — high drama, very controlled. Light mode: the beams become like sunlight through a window into a pale room, the grid like faint graph paper — architectural, quiet, precise. The metaphor: the moment a hidden risk becomes visible. Clarity from darkness. The most abstract and the most conceptually resonant direction here.

Mood: revelatory · architectural precision · clarity from darkness
